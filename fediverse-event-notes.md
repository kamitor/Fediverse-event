# Fediverse Event Notes

## Event Overview
Nodes on a Web (NOAW) brings together Fediverse builders and public institutions to explore sustainable ActivityPub deployments. The 2026 unconference centers on transparency, privacy, and practical collaboration between EU public-sector teams and Fediverse developers.

### Highlights
- FediVariety launches findings from the EDPS EU Voice/Video pilot.
- Format: unconference with community-driven sessions plus kickoff keynote.
- Audience: developers, admins, civic technologists, communications staff, policy teams.
- Goals: align roadmaps, share moderation/governance patterns, plan long-term support.

### Logistics
- Dates: 19–20 March 2026
- Location: Marinebasis, Amsterdam
- Registration: officially closed (contact Billie for exceptions)
- Supported by: CIO-Rijk (Ministry of the Interior & Kingdom Relations), City of Amsterdam Digitalisation & Innovation, NLnet, SABOA.
